Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book VS Film for my wedding?
+
The easiest way is to WhatsApp Satish directly at +91 98246 23330 with your event date, type, and location. You can also visit our studio at 440 Leonard Square, Near Yogi Chowk, Surat. We respond within a few hours!
How soon are photos delivered after the event?
+
Edited photos are typically delivered within 2–3 weeks. Cinematic highlight reels and full video edits are delivered within 4–6 weeks, depending on your package. We'll keep you updated throughout the editing process via WhatsApp.
Do you travel outside Surat for shoots?
+
Absolutely! We regularly travel across Gujarat and India for destination weddings and pre-wedding shoots. Travel costs are added to the package at actual rates. Popular destinations include Rajasthan, Goa, Udaipur, and international locations.
What is the booking advance amount?
+
We require a small advance to secure your date in our calendar. The exact amount depends on your package and event. WhatsApp us to discuss — we're flexible and want to make it easy for you to book your dream photographer!
Can I get a custom package for my event?
+
Yes, absolutely. Our packages are starting points — we customise every booking based on your event type, number of days, team size required, and deliverables. WhatsApp Satish with your requirements for a personalised quote.
Where is the VS Film studio in Surat?
+
Our studio is at 440 Leonard Square, Near Yogi Chowk, Savlia Circle, Yogi Chowk Road, Vijay Nagar 2, Yoginagar Society, Surat, Gujarat 395011. Easy to find near Yogi Chowk — a well-known landmark in Surat. Get Directions →
